About me

I am a driven, determined and eager to learn individual. I was originally taught to code at Makers Academy, where I was not only taught how to code, but also taught the great principles surrounding code. Coding principles such as Test Driven Development(TDD), the SOLID principles and Agile methodologies were taught as funadamental to being a good and successful software developer. At Makers Academy, we undertook different projects every week, often utilising different frameworks, as we expanded our knowledge base daily. Pair programming on all projects was required from day one. This allowed me to develop my team working skills, and learn more readily through seeing other peoples approaches to programming and understanding how other people went about software craftsmanship. During our final projects, we were required to work in teams of five, on a two week project. This included scrumming twice a day, splitting the work load amongst our team, and organising the project via Trello.
